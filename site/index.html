The auction

Launched by clearing auction. Everyone paid the same path.

$SCRATCH launched by continuous clearing auction — Uniswap's onchain fair-launch mechanism. No snipers, no insider allocations, no dev candle. Bids settled at one uniform clearing price, then liquidity deployed automatically and trading began.

01

Bids in ETH

Bids were placed at any price at or above the floor during the 12-hour window. Bids were onchain and refundable if the auction didn't clear.

02

One clearing price

At close, the auction found the single price where demand met supply. Everyone above it won at exactly that price. Everyone below was refunded.

03

Liquidity deployed itself

The pool launched at the clearing price with a dense USDG shelf under it and the LP position locked for a year — the lock was verifiable before bidding.
